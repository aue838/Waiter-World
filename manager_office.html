📖 Restaurant Case Law

Courts tend to favour common decency and fairness. Think before you speak. Think before you act. Are you decent? That's the (potentially) $1.75 dollar million question!

A non-exhaustive list of rulings pertaining to restaurant work. Laws differ by regions so be sure to look up yours!

Bedtime Stories for our Friends in the Service Industry

    Over-serving - Never a Good Idea

  • 18 shots, 3 hrs 45 min = one fatality, 60 day suspension of liquor licence
  • 17 beers, four hrs, one patron = one death, 60 day suspension of liquor licence
  • 4 pitchers, 2 patrons, 2 hours = one death, 45 day suspension of liquor licence
  • 10 beers, one patron = three deaths, two injured, $400, 000 damages
  • One intoxicated driver, one passenger = quadriplegia and $1.75 million dollars

    • Be Nice

  • Verbal hostility, spreading rumours, systemic undermining = 18 months full salary plus $15, 000 aggravated damages plus 'special damages'
  • False defamatory rumours, professional sabotage = 9 months full severance plus $10, 000 aggravated damage plus $60, 000 specifically for defamation

    • Be Nice to the Boss

  • Employee walked off the job claiming unfair environment = Restaurant won - voluntary resignation, no damages awarded
  • Employee 'jumps the gun' by quitting and suing = Judge rules in favour of the restaurant, no damages

    • Always Play Fair

  • Forced demotion, cut in pay, senior worker = 20 months full salary
  • Threatened with dismissal when leaving for pre-approved time off = $70, 025 plus $20, 000 in punitive damages paid by owner of restaurant

👔 Appearance & Punctuality

  • Dress Code: black pants, black shoes, and black socks.
  • Hair: Must be tied back, off the face, and off the shoulders.
  • Punctuality: Clock in before your scheduled start time.
  • Harassment: No swearing/yelling; civility is a legal requirement.
  • Poisoned Work Environment: The employer can be held liable.

Always remember, regardless of your position we are all here for one reason - to earn a living. Let's work well together!
